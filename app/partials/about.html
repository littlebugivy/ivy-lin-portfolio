About Ivy

Hi. My name is Mengdie Lin and I go by Ivy. I am a first-year master student from the School of Information, University of Michigan specialized in Human-computer Interaction (HCI). Before that, I studied computer science at the University of Nottingham (two wonderful years in Ningbo, China and another two in Nottingham, UK).

I am a tech advancer and a user experience designer. Besides developing software to serve people's needs, I care about how people think of and interact with technologies. I am excited about exploring emerging technologies such as AR/VR, automobiles, smart homes but also dedicated to improving experience around websites, mobile applications and online services used on daily basis. I believe that technologies could better facilitate human activities and bring real convenience and joy. I wish more people enjoy technologies, and enjoy it more.

Besides the professional side of me, I am a person born with curiosity. You may find me always trying and learning new things. I fill my stomach with delicious food from different areas and feed my brain with articles, books, music, movies and stories from strangers. I love travelling and has been to nine countries.
