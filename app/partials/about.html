About Ivy

Hi. My name is Mengdie Lin and I go by Ivy. I am a first-year master student from the School of Information, University of Michigan specialized in Human-computer Interaction (HCI). Before that, I studied computer science at the University of Nottingham (two wonderful years in Ningbo, China and two unforgettable years in Nottingham, UK).

I am a tech advancer and a user experience designer. Besides developing software to serve people's needs, I care about how people think of and interact with technologies. I am excited about exploring emerging technologies such as AR/VR, automobiles, smart homes but also dedicated to improving experience around websites, mobile applications and online services which are used on daily basis. I believe that technologies could better facilitate human activities and bring convenience and joy. I wish more people enjoy technologies, and enjoy it more.

Besides the professional side of me, I am a person born with curiosity who loves trying and learning new things. I feed my stomach with delicious food discovered via different methods and feed my brain with articles, books, music, movies and stories from strangers. I love travelling and has been to nine countries. I love jogging and have completed a half marathon last year. FYI. I also have a cat named Orio.
